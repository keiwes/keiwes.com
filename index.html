Axure

I have trained and supported development teams with Axure and the best practices of wireframing and rapid prototyping. "Getting Started with Axure" is an instructional video that teaches the major features of the application in less than 30 minutes. See it on YouTube

My Work

My Work is an employee dashboard designed to increase productivity by providing tools in one location. This dashboard was built in SharePoint as an extension to the My Site functionality. Company web parts and external website web parts were available and customizable. A notification bar was included to alert employees when they needed to perform a work action, or when they received private messages. Brand colors were used.

Singing Colors

Singing Colors is a simple, educational Android app for toddlers and young children to help with learning colors. When a color is selected, it animates to fill the screen, spells the color, and the color is sung as part of the musical scale. I created Singing Colors from concept to publication to learn more about Android design and development. See it on Google Play

UX Class

I taught user experience classes to incoming IT interns. I reviewed the UX field and careers, common web practices, the psychology behind how we use the web, different approaches and tools for wireframing and prototyping, qualitative usability testing, and the company brand.
